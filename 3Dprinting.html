My 3D Printing Projects

My passion for 3D printing has been longstanding. The idea that you could tell a machine to create something and it was create it without any help seems like an idea of the future and yet it is happening today. I got my first 3D printer at the end of 2019, this was a gift from my aunt. (Love you Aunt Marci). After I got the printer I started to print different things. These were just nicknacks that I had found online. After this I started to model different things in Fusion 360. These ranged from handgrips for OT's to stands for my phone.
